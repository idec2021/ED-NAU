Introduction

As one of the first teams to join the iDEC competition, we showed great honor to find some partners and communicate with them.

They reminded us that we are not a single team but a traveler who will finally meet partners with a common goal.

NAU-China

Being our brother team, iGEM from Nanjing Agriculture University provided us critical help. We shared the same lab and meeting room, we chatted with each other and supported each other. It is the iGEMers’ support that gave us such motivation to complete the whole experiment.

University of Chinese Academy of Sciences

On 5th September, iGEM from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences and our team hold a meeting about the use of error-prone PCR and directed evolution online. We reached an agreement that deep communication will be maintained.

Ocean University Of China

As the first team to join the iDEC competition, the iDEC team from ocean university of China communicated with us on 29th September, we share our project work with each other and reminded each other the matters about the deadline.
